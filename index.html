About Us

Introduction

Community Services of Minnesota Inc. is a conglomerate of services delegated to aid individuals and small businesses to be the best version of themselves by “piecing together the pinnacle of success". It is owned and operated by Larry Ajala, located in Anoka Minnesota, Larry Recently celebrated 10+ years working in human services field as a combined 10 years + of 245D/Assisted living service experience between him and his team. The mission of Community Services Of Minnesota as coach Larry is to provide fast and reliable services in auditing, consulting, coaching, and staff development.
